Hi, I'm currently an undergraduate student at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, pursuing a Bachelor of Fine Arts in New Media.

I am passionate about the crossroads between technology, design and culture. I aspire to make technology easily accessible through user-friendly design and interface in an effort to improve efficiency, design, and user retention.

As a new media artist, I challenge the separation of engineering and fine arts by merging technology and design together, forcing them to become interdependent. I often experiement with different digital techniques in order to create unique experiences.
