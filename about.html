Tony has graduated from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in New Media, concentrating in Interaction Design. He is passionate about UI/ UX, interaction design, technology, and problem solving.

Tony has developed a core set of forms that incorporate both established and emerging technologies such as time-based media, performance, digital imaging, coding, and interactive technologies.

He explores the integration of new emerging technology, design, and culture. Through the use of his cross-disciplinary field of study, he is also able to investigate different problems through different interactive mediums such as mobile apps, VR, AR, web-technologies, and creative coding. He aspires to make user-centered design in order to improve efficiency, usability, and user retention.

In his free time, he enjoys experimenting with computational art through coding, videography, traveling, and cooking.
