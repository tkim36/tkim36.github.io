silent disco inspiration

D E F I N E

The problem we try to address while listening to music is that listening to music has become an independent/ isolated activity that usually disconnects people from the world around them. Our solution was to attempt to make listening to music a social activity (if wanted) that allows people to be connected with their current surrounding by experiencing the different musical culture each location contains.

Our defined territory and user base is a college campus with college students. As for data collection/ retrieval, the data will revolve around mostly compressed audio files. Other data that will be collected are user profiles, which can be customized, as well as chat logs which are location specific.

D E V E L O P

The low-fidelity mockups are designed so that the main feature, which is the microradio feature, is placed in the middle. The other buttoms/ tabs are placed along the bottom in order for the user to have easy access since the buttons will be near their thumbs. The maps are designed so that the areas with the "sound" territories are indicated. The larger the community/ collection of songs, the larger the radius of a certain territoy.
