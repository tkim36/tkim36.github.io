Hawker Sensor

Mobile UI / UX Case Study

Hawker Sensor is a case study that utilizes the data set from the National Environment Agency in Singapore that measures information about Hawker centers, which are public food courts in Singapore. The case study analyzes two sources and conceptualizes a location based mobile application that allows the selected target audience to keep track of their culinary experience in Singaporean hawker centers.

LumiNUS Redesign Case Study

UX Redesign Case Study

LumiNUS is the new NUS (National University of Singapore) student portal that was officially implemented in the beginning of AY19/20. It was introduced as a replacement for the old student portal, IVLE. LumiNUS aims to act as all NUS students's one stop portal for mainly downloading lecture and tutorial materials, submitting assignments as well as receiving announcements from lecturers.
